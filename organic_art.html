ECO PRINTING

HOW TO MAKE ECO PRINTS ?

STEP 1

Collect all seasonally available flowers, plants and kitchen waste. Kitchen scraps are actually my favorite to use because they would otherwise go to waste. I also really like to collect dying flowers from my garden and from my neighbors. Also some salt and vinegar.

STEP 2

Place material on cloth, but really you can do it however you like. Play around with it and have fun!

STEP 3

After you've placed the material onto your fabric it's time to roll it up tightly and secure with twine or a rubber band. Then you simply steam it. So easy! I typically let my bundles steam for 1-2 hours and then rest over night.

STEP 4

Take out the clothes and let them dry. So there you have it.

You can do eco printing on papers , cotton cloth and silk cloths as well.
